    What Is Settlement Hierarchy?

    Settlement Hierarchy refers to a methodology for classifying human settlements using population, density and/or other relevant criteria to determine logical groupings. Though this criteria can be nuanced and complex, the simplest approach solely uses population counts to determine settlement categories. This project uses open data from GeoNames.org to classify and visualize all locations on Earth with populations over 5,000 into six categories: Megalopolis, Metropolis, Large City, City, Large Town, and Town. Enter a city in the search field above to find its settlement hierarchy classification.
